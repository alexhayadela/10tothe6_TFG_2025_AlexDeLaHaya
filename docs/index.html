Signal Definition

The model estimates the probability that the IBEX 35 will close positive on the next trading day.

Output: P(Returnt+1 > 0)

Model Architecture

  • Algorithm: Random Forest (Tree Ensemble)
  • Forecast Horizon: 1 Day
  • Training Scheme: Expanding Window
  • Lookahead Bias: Eliminated

Signal Selection Logic

Assets shown in daily email are selected by confidence score:

Score = | P − 0.5 |

The top 3 assets with highest absolute deviation from 0.5 are displayed as highest conviction signals.
